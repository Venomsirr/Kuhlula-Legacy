Company Overview

Kuhlula Legacy is moving your world forward.

Kuhlula Legacy is a diversified private company specializing in logistics, plant hire, and supply chain solutions. Headquartered in Gauteng, South Africa, we deliver reliable, efficient, and cost-effective transport and equipment solutions to the construction, mining, and industrial sectors. We operate a fleet that includes Front-End Loaders, Graders, TLBs, Horse & Trailer trucks, and Light to Heavy Goods transport vehicles. Our mission is to strengthen client operations and contribute to socio-economic growth through ethical practices and operational excellence.
